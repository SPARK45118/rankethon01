About Us

At Rankethon, we believe the future belongs to the curious. We are a student-centric startup on a mission to spark innovation, creativity, and tech curiosity among school students. By organizing hands-on workshops, interactive tech events, and fun learning experiences, Rankethon bridges the gap between traditional education and the dynamic world of technology.

What We Do

We provide students with an opportunity to dive deep into the world of technology by conducting workshops that cover a wide range of exciting tech topics. Whether it's coding, robotics, web development, or artificial intelligence, our workshops are designed to foster curiosity and creativity in young minds.

Tech Workshops

Hands-on sessions where students can learn about the latest in tech, from programming languages to building robots.

Competitions & Challenges

Exciting tech competitions to fuel students' creativity and inspire them to apply their skills.

Tech Events

Exclusive events that give students the chance to showcase their talents, network with industry professionals, and gain recognition.
